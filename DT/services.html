Services

Paintless Dent Repair (PDR)

Features

Efficiently removes minor dents, dings, and creases without the need for repainting or body filler.

Hail Damage Repair:

Features

Specialized services to restore vehicles affected by hailstorms, repairing multiple dents while preserving the original paint.

Door Ding Repair:

Features

Quick and affordable fixes for small dents and dings caused by door impacts in parking lots.

Aluminum Panel Dent Repair:

Features

Advanced PDR techniques tailored to aluminum panels, which require specific tools and expertise due to their unique properties.

Large Dent Removal:

Features

Skilled repair of larger or more complex dents that do not compromise the paint, restoring the vehicle's appearance.

Lease Return Repairs:

Features

Pre-return dent removal to help customers avoid costly fees for damages when returning leased vehicles.

Mobile Dent Repair Services:

Features

Convenient, on-site dent repair where technicians travel to the customer's location to complete repairs.

Fleet Vehicle Maintenance:

Features

Comprehensive dent removal and maintenance services for company fleets to keep vehicles in pristine condition and protect their value.
