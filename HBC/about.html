About Heavenbound Customs

What drives Heavenbound Customs?

We are driven by a deep passion for diesel performance and a commitment to serving our customers and community.

Community Driven

Heavenbound Customs is proud to be a local business rooted in the community. We believe in fostering relationships, supporting local events, and giving back whenever we can. Building trust with our customers and neighbors is the cornerstone of our success.

Locally Focused

We proudly serve our local and regional customers with unmatched dedication and expertise. Our focus on the area we know best allows us to tailor our services to the specific needs of our customers.

Transparent

At Heavenbound Customs, transparency is key. From initial quotes to the final handshake, we ensure our customers understand every step of the process. No hidden fees, no surprises—just honest, reliable service you can count on.

Faith Oriented

Faith is at the heart of everything we do. Our work reflects our belief in integrity, kindness, and stewardship, striving to glorify God through our actions and the quality of our service.

Our mission

Delivering Excellence, Built on Faith Our mission is to provide industry-leading craftsmanship and unparalleled service while staying true to our values. At Heavenbound Customs, we aim to:

  • Transform your diesel truck into the best version of itself.
  • Build lasting relationships with our customers based on trust and respect.
  • Use our talents to serve both our community and God.
  • Provide reliable, innovative solutions that keep you on the road and ahead of the curve.

At Heavenbound Customs, we specialize in delivering premium customizations, repairs, and maintenance for diesel trucks. Built on a foundation of integrity, craftsmanship, and faith, we take pride in serving our customers with the highest level of expertise and care. Our passion lies in transforming your vision into reality—whether it's enhancing performance, improving efficiency, or creating a one-of-a-kind build.
